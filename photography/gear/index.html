Photography Gear

For my camera I use a Sony a7 full frame camera. I also have a battery grip attached to it for longer battery life. For lenses I mostly use vintage lenses (I only have one modern lens). Vintage lenses are cheap and can still produce high quality artistic images. I also like shooting with manual focus (I even use manual focus on my modern lens), as I feel it gives me more control over how the image will come out (and also I'm just more used to manual focus).
